Mini-FAQ

How much time do we need?
Even 15–20 minutes per session works great. Pick one topic, take notes, quiz each other.
Do we need money to start?
Not at all. You don't need to start jumping into investing money in real companies until you have a solid understanding of what the stock market is about.
What should we cover first?
Start with the basics: Introduction, Types of Stocks, and Risk & Reward. Then compare Investing vs Trading.
