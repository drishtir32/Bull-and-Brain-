🏦 Market Players

Learn about the different participants in the stock market and their unique roles.

👩‍💻 Retail Investors

  • Individuals trading with personal money.
  • Use apps like Zerodha, Groww, Robinhood.
  • Examples: You, Me, Beginner traders.

🏢 Institutional Investors

  • Large organizations investing pooled money.
  • Mutual funds, hedge funds, pension funds.
  • Examples: BlackRock, Vanguard, LIC.

📊 Brokers

  • Middlemen between buyers and sellers.
  • Provide trading platforms and research.
  • Examples: Zerodha, Fidelity, Charles Schwab.

💰 Market Makers

  • Provide liquidity by quoting buy & sell prices.
  • Ensure smoother transactions in the market.
  • Examples: Citadel Securities, Virtu Financial.

⚖ Regulators

  • Protect investors and ensure fair play.
  • Make and enforce market rules.
  • Examples: SEBI (India), SEC (US), FCA (UK).
