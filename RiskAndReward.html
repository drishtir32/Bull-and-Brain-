⚖️ Risk & Reward

Understanding how risks and rewards work is key to making smart investment decisions.

🔴 What is Risk?

  • The chance of losing some or all of your investment.
  • Higher risk usually means higher uncertainty.
  • Examples: stock price crashes, company failures, global crises.

🟢 What is Reward?

  • The potential profit you earn from an investment.
  • Comes in the form of dividends, interest, or capital gains.
  • Example: stock value doubling, receiving yearly dividends.

⚖️ Risk-Reward Tradeoff

  • Investors must balance how much risk they are willing to take for the potential return.
  • Low risk = usually lower returns (like savings accounts).
  • High risk = chance for higher returns (like stocks, crypto).

📊 Types of Investment Risk

  • Market Risk – prices fluctuate.
  • Credit Risk – borrower may default.
  • Liquidity Risk – difficulty selling quickly.
  • Inflation Risk – money loses value over time.

🌍 Managing Risk

  • Diversify your investments across sectors.
  • Invest only what you can afford to lose.
  • Set stop-loss orders and keep emergency funds.
