🌟 My Overall Journey Reflection

This page is dedicated to my overall thoughts and reflections on my journey.

My Learning Reflection

I learnt a lot during this 6 week journey, ranging from deepening my knowledge to building upon concepts that I never knew about. It was difficult to grasp some concepts, but I pushed through and figured it out. I also learned how to prompt ChatGPT (after typing in all caps).

Challenges I Faced

The main cahllenges I faced was prompting ChatGPT to do the right thing. Since I had so many files and wanted the same navigation bar across all files, prompting ChatGPT to make the navigation bar consistent was like arguing with a wall, but it all worked out at the end of the day.

How I Improved

I learnt a lot more about the HTML syntax and how to prompt ChatGPT, of course. I also gained a lot more valuable knowledge about the stock market. So, it was a three-in-one project!

Final Takeaways

I learnt a lot from this project and gained a lot of knowledge that I didn't know about before. It involved arguing with ChatGPT, but I can't wait for the next project I will make! Who knows, I might just make this site big and publish it on the internet!
