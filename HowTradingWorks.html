💹 How Trading Works

This lesson explains in simple words how trading happens, the types of orders you can use, strategies, and beginner tips.

📈 Market Orders

  • Buy or sell stocks immediately at the current price.
  • Fast and simple for beginners.
  • Good if you want to make quick trades.

⏱ Limit Orders

  • Set the exact price at which you want to buy or sell.
  • The order happens only if the market reaches that price.
  • Helps control your trades and avoid overpaying.

💡 Trading Strategies

  • Day trading: buy and sell within the same day.
  • Swing trading: hold for a few days to weeks.
  • Long-term investing: hold for months or years.

📝 Tips for Beginners

  • Start small and practice before investing big.
  • Do research before buying any stock.
  • Focus on long-term goals, not small price changes.
  • Separate emotions from trading decisions.
  • Use limit orders to avoid overpaying.
